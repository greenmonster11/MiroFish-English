=== SITUATION OVERVIEW ===

The United States and Israel launched a joint military campaign against Iran on February 28, 2026. The conflict has rapidly escalated, with Iran's Islamic Revolutionary Guard Corps (IRGC) effectively closing the Strait of Hormuz — through which 20% of the world's oil supply transits. Iran's new Supreme Leader has declared the strait should "remain shut" and warned that "not one litre of oil" will pass through. Vessels linked to the U.S., Israel, or their allies are considered "legitimate targets."

The IEA released 400 million barrels from emergency reserves — the largest release in history — but the impact on oil prices has been limited. Iran's IRGC has warned to "expect oil at $200 per barrel" if attacks on its energy infrastructure continue.
